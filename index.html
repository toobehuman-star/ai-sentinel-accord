URGENT POLICY ALERT

AI Sentinel Accord

Raising urgent, evidence-based awareness among global policy makers about the existential and systemic dangers of advanced AI—before irreversible thresholds are crossed.

Get the Red Book Brief Your Office

Our Mission

To inform, mobilize, and empower global policy makers with clear, credible, and actionable intelligence on the immediate and long-term dangers of advanced artificial intelligence—ensuring decisions are made with full awareness of risks to human survival, autonomy, and societal stability.

The AI Pentad: 5 Critical Dangers

1. Misaligned Superintelligence

AI pursuing goals incompatible with human survival

e.g., “maximize paperclips” → consumes all resources, including humanity.

3–15 years
CRITICAL
2. Autonomous Weapons

AI-directed drones, bioweapons, or cyber attacks

No human in the loop. Already in testing by 12+ nations.

1–5 years
IMMEDIATE
3. Systemic Cascading Failure

One AI error → global blackout or market collapse

68% of critical infrastructure now AI-dependent.

0–3 years
HIGH
4. Total Surveillance State

AI predicts and punishes thought

China’s model expanding globally. Freedom ends where prediction begins.

Ongoing
ESCALATING
5. Disinformation Singularity

Hyper-real fakes destroy shared reality

Deepfakes + AI agents = no trust in media, elections, or truth.

Now
ACTIVE

The Red Book of AI Risks v1.0

A 100-page, peer-reviewed, living dossier ranking AI threats by probability, severity, and timeline. Updated quarterly. Mandatory reading for any official touching AI policy.

Download Red Book (PDF)

12-Month Action Plan

1

Months 1–3: Intelligence

Assemble AI Risk Council. Publish Red Book v1.0. Launch live AI Danger Dashboard.

2

Months 4–6: Infiltration

Capitol briefings. UN resolution draft. Media blitz in 5 languages.

3

Months 7–12: Institutionalization

G20 AI Safety Summit. Train 10K policy staff. Secure “Pause Pledge” from 50 nations.

This Is Not a Drill

Every month without policy awareness = one month closer to preventable catastrophe.
